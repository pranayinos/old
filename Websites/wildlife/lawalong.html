lawalong WildLife Sanctuary

Introduction

Located in Chatra district it has a forest area of 211 sq. km. Lawalong provides a variety of wildlife that includes many species of mammals, birds and reptiles.

    Important Mammals

    Etiam Leopard, Hyena, Spotted Deer, Wild boar, Bear, Sambhar, Barking Deer, Porcupine, Indian Pangolin,Rhesus Monkey, Mongoose, Jackal, Fox, Common otteretc.





    Important Reptiles

    Chameleon Python, Cobra, Common Turtle.

    Importanat Birds

    Hoopoe, Bee Eater, Jungle Myna, Red Vented Bulbul, Black Headed Oriole, Barbet, Pied Kingfisher etc.





    Important Rivers

    Amanat, Chako, Liazan, Barhed Nala.

    Tourist Facilities

    Forest Rest House at Chatra and Lawalong




    How to reach

    Nearesr towns are Chatra and Hazaribag, Nearest railway Point is Tori about 66km.




    When to visit

    October to March.
